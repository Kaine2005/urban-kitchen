Our Story
We didn't open a restaurant. We opened a reason to go out.

Urban Kitchen started as a takeaway. Now it is becoming something Arbroath has never had. A proper sit-down boutique restaurant on Lochlands Street. Real food, a serious bar, and a beer garden to match. Built from scratch and built to stay.

5% mince. Real ingredients. Everything fresh. No exceptions.
